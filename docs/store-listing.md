## Full description (≤4000 chars)
FocusFlow is a calm, all-in-one productivity app for building habits, doing focused work, and tracking the goals that matter. Everything runs on your device. Nothing is uploaded. There are no accounts to create and no ads.

**Habits**
· Binary habits (done / not done) and counter habits (steps, glasses of water, pages read)
· Tap to increment, double-tap to complete, long-press to reset
· Tiered streak system — streaks promote as you build them
· Positive and negative habit tracking linked to your profile
· Optional mini-journal prompt when you complete a habit

**Deep Work & Timers**
· Pomodoro with customizable work and break lengths
· Stopwatch for open-ended sessions
· Fullscreen timer with wake-lock and end chime
· Link a timer to a task or goal — minutes accrue automatically
· Ambient brown-noise audio with low-frequency oscillation

**Meditation**
· Guided breath presets (box, 4-7-8, resonant)
· Custom interval timers
· Heatmap and streak tracking

**Projects, Tasks & Goals**
· Nested task trees with priority, due date, and progress
· Goals link to real tasks — progress rings update as tasks complete
· Optional minute targets so deep-work sessions move the goal ring
· Dashboard shows overdue, high-priority, and today's work at a glance

**Fitness**
· Weightlifting with sets/reps/weight and automatic PR detection
· Cardio with distance, duration, and pace
· Martial arts rounds, custom modalities — all link to goals

**Journal**
· Plain journal for daily reflections
· Optional passcode protection — entries encrypted on-device with AES-GCM (key derived via PBKDF2 250k iterations)
· Passcode is never sent anywhere; reset wipes entries

**Privacy-first**
· All data stored locally (IndexedDB + local storage)
· Backup to a file you control — export to any cloud, email, or disk
· Optional AI assistant uses Pollinations; off by default
· No analytics, no tracking, no advertising

**Works offline**
FocusFlow installs as a PWA and runs fully offline after the first open. Notifications, haptics, and keep-awake are native where available.

Built for people who want a quiet tool that respects their attention and their data.
